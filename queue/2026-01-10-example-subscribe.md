# Example subscribe post

## Пост 1
Most people don't quit their note app because it's bad.

They quit because opening it became a tiny chore — three taps, a sync spinner, a blank page asking "what's on your mind?"

The blank page is the enemy. Not the features.

The tools that win are the ones that already have a half-finished thought waiting when you open them.
